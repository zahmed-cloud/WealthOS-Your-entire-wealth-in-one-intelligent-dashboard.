FAQ

Frequently asked.

Where is my data stored?▼
Entirely in your browser, via localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. We have zero access to your data — and neither does anyone else.
What asset types are supported?▼
Stocks, ETFs, real estate, cryptocurrency, art, watches, cash, and savings. WealthOS was built for multi-asset portfolios — not retrofitted from a stock tracker.
What does Pro include beyond Free?▼
Free tracks up to 5 assets with a basic dashboard. Pro removes that limit and adds AI intelligence, risk alerts, projections, market updates, quarterly reports, and data export. Private adds stress testing, multi-user access, and advisor sharing.
Does it connect to my brokerage or bank?▼
No — values are entered manually. No account credentials ever required. Brokerage sync is on the roadmap.
